## Residual risk after mitigation

RISK-W02-001 and RISK-W02-004 are expected to reduce to low residual risk once their respective
mitigations (bounded interim-lease surface; investigation-before-implementation sequencing) are
executed as planned. RISK-W02-002 cannot be pre-resolved by this wave's planning — its outcome is a
fact about the actual data, discovered only when the audit runs — and is tracked as a genuine
blocking risk to E02-S002's closure, not merely a documentation item. RISK-W02-003 is accepted as an
irreducible-within-this-wave residual risk per PLAN's own explicit framing.
