## Residual risk after mitigation

RISK-W05-001 and RISK-W05-005 are expected to reduce to low residual risk once their respective
adversarial/completeness proofs are genuinely executed, not merely implemented — both carry an
independent-review task for exactly this reason. RISK-W05-002 reduces to low once T6's declaration-
class enumeration is explicit and reviewed. RISK-W05-003 and RISK-W05-004 are schedule- and
coordination-conditional risks that this wave's own internal sequencing (E05 last, S001-before-S002
within E05) is designed to manage but cannot fully eliminate — both remain open until E05 actually
executes and wowsociety's identity suite is confirmed green.
