## Residual risk after mitigation

RISK-W04-001 and RISK-W04-003 are expected to reduce to low residual risk once their respective
mitigations (the explicit checkpoint-migration step; the recorded breaking-change coordination
note) are executed as planned. RISK-W04-002 cannot be fully resolved within this wave's own
closure — its full resolution requires the product-side PROD-05 staging drill, tracked but not
owned by this wave. RISK-W04-004 is expected to remain open at this wave's closure by design — it
resolves only once W05-E03-S002 lands and a follow-on task implements DX-07 T4, which this wave
explicitly does not attempt.
